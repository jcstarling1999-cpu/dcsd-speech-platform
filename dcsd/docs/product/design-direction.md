# Design Direction — Auralith Atlas

Last updated: 2026-04-15

## Three Art Directions
1. Auralith Atlas (selected)
- Editorial serif + geometric sans pairing.
- Maritime-mineral palette and layered glass control chrome.
- Balances precision and warmth.

2. Signal Forge
- Industrial grid, dense operator-console aesthetics.
- High contrast and utilitarian motion language.

3. Quiet Broadcast
- Soft print-inspired tones and annotation-like typography.
- Emphasizes long-form listening and accessibility workflows.

## Selected System: Auralith Atlas
- Typography:
  - Headline: Fraunces
  - UI/body: Space Grotesk
- Color semantics:
  - Coral: immediate action
  - Amber: warnings/lossy paths
  - Lagoon: successful readiness
- Shape language:
  - Capsule controls, asymmetrical panel rhythms
- Motion language:
  - Staggered rise transitions and clarity-first state cues

## Non-Sameness Checklist
- Silhouette uniqueness: avoid template hero/card rhythm.
- Typography distinctiveness: enforced serif + geometric pairing.
- Interaction originality: progressive artifacts and queue telemetry integrated into primary UI.
- Brand voice: operationally precise, no hype filler.

## Liquid Glass Usage Rules
- Apple-native mode: use Liquid Glass in navigation/control layers, not dense content.
- Web mode: use emulated glass tokens (`blur`, `tint`, `edge`, `shadow`) with reduced-motion and non-backdrop fallbacks.
